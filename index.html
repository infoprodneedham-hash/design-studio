Terms of Service

Welcome to Geelong Design Studio. Please review our baseline arrangements regarding digital projects, ongoing management, and development agreements.

1. Quotes, Pricing & Payment Terms

All structured adjustments, functional add-ons, or asset productions are quoted clearly upfront before asset execution begins.

  • Deposit: A 50% upfront payment is mandatory to lock in production windows for custom web architectures.
  • Milestones: Outstanding project balances are due strictly upon localized asset sign-off or prior to live hosting migration.
  • Validity: Formulated custom quotes remain valid for exactly 30 calendar days from the production issue date.

2. Scope of Responsibility

We pride ourselves on offering flexible, tailored agreements matching unique customer parameters while respecting technical operational clear-lines.

  • Client Inputs: Clients agree to deliver accurate copy guidelines, photography content assets, and required local accessibility metrics promptly.
  • Revisions: Every custom web project includes up to two rounds of creative iterations within the base-quoted parameters unless explicitly noted otherwise.
  • Third-Party Accounts: Registering and funding domains, ongoing serverless backends, or premium newsletter platforms remains the financial responsibility of the client.

3. Client Expectations

Timely production depends entirely on balanced collaboration. Delays in asset sign-off or structural content handovers exceeding 14 days may result in rescheduling the project sequence or adjusting pricing frameworks accordingly.
